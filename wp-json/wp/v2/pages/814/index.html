This brief bibliography lists scholarly works useful for investigating the history and legacies of racial inequality at the University of Delaware and its surrounding communities. Reflecting the research sponsored by the Legacies committee to date, it focuses on the nineteenth century. When possible, it provides links to online versions of resources. Neither exhaustive nor comprehensive, this list is intended to be a starting point \u2013 not an end.<\/p>\n
